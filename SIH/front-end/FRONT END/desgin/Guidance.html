Points for Discovering Your Child's Potential:

  • Encourage curiosity by exposing your child to diverse subjects and activities.
  • Observe your child's natural interests and strengths to identify potential talents.
  • Provide opportunities for creative problem-solving to enhance critical thinking.
  • Support their efforts, regardless of the outcome, to build self-confidence.
  • Promote a growth mindset by praising hard work and persistence over innate ability.
  • Help them set realistic goals and celebrate small achievements.
  • Maintain open communication to understand their passions and aspirations.
  • Provide constructive feedback focused on improvement, not perfection.
